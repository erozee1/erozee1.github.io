Overview


During a summer internship, I had the privilege of working as a Mechanical Design Engineer and Technical Project Manager at a company specializing in testing and assembling powertrain solutions.


In this role, I was immersed in the world of complex part design using SolidWorks, where I created innovative and efficient solutions.


When I moved to the shop floor I was asigned to a project that was building a unit designed to test the housing for vehicle batteries.


Throughout these projects multiple issues arose that make the experience challenging and exciting.



Skills I developed:


  • Working under an expierenced team of mechanical engineers.
  • Communicating with shop floor mechanics to solve problems on the go.
  • Implimenting SolidWorks into real world parts.
  • A focus on Design for Manufacture and Assembly.
